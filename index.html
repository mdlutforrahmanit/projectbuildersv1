Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our construction services.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on size and complexity. A small residential renovation might take 2-3 months, while a large commercial project could require 12-18 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific project requirements.

What's included in your construction costs?

Our transparent pricing includes all labor, materials, equipment, permits, and project management. We provide detailed breakdowns in our estimates so you understand exactly what you're paying for. There are no hidden fees - any changes during the project are documented in change orders with clear pricing.

How do you handle project delays?

We build contingency time into all schedules to account for weather or unforeseen issues. Our project managers maintain constant communication if delays occur, providing alternative solutions to minimize impact. 98% of our projects complete on or ahead of the adjusted schedule.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer comprehensive warranties ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the project type and materials used. All warranties are fully documented and we conduct follow-up inspections to ensure everything meets our quality standards long after project completion.

How sustainable are your building practices?

Sustainability is core to our operations. We prioritize energy-efficient designs, recycled materials, and waste reduction processes. Over 75% of our projects achieve LEED certification. Our team stays current with the latest green building technologies to minimize environmental impact without compromising quality.
